Certificate Requirements

Students completing the certificate shall have earned a grade of C or better in each of the required courses. At least 15 credit hours are required to earn the certificate, as described below.

Step 1: Foundational Informatics Course (3 Hours)

INFO 2000 - Informatics 1

Step 2:

Pick at least 2 of these classes to fulfill this requirement

Additional Courses (at least 6 Credit Hours)

Pre-Req: Permission of Department

Pre-Req: MATH1113 or Permission of Department
